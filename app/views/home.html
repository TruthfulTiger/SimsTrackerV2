This is a game helper, specifically for players of The Sims series (currently just 2) to keep track of their sims, and even keep score of a legacy challenge (more info at Sims Legacy Challenge).

This is the first version, which I'm keeping online as a showcase; version 2 is being worked on offline and includes a redesign of the back-end. Will update with link once it's ready for testing.

Most of the magic happens when you are logged in, so feel free to create an account and have a play 🙂

Last updated: 16/11/2022

Finished: Sims 2 Aspiration and family generators; Sims 2 Legacy Scoresheet; Sims 2 tracker (although "view Sim" is incomplete)

WIP: Complete redesign, mainly of database side, to better accommodate Sims 3 and 4. This version is still up for posterity, but works fine for tracking gameplay in Sims 2.

Updated: Fixed bugs with "create hood" and choosing careers in "update Sim". "View Sim" now shows career details (where applicable), including skills and friends needed to progress.
